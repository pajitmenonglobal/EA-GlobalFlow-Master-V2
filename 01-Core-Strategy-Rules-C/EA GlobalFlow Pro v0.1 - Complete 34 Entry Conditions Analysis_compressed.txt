##	Notation System Reference ### Bar Reference System
- 'Indicator(-n) = n bars behind current bar
- 'Indicator(0) = current bar
- Indicator(1) = 1 bar behind (previous bar)
- Indicator(+26) = 26 bars ahead of current

### Comparison Operators
- = equal to
- '> is greater than
- '< is lesser than
- '<=' or =<' is equal to or less than
- =>' or ' >=' is equal to or greater than

### Cross Operators (CRITICAL)
- 'x< or <x is **crosses below from above**
- 'x› or '>x is **crosses above from below**

### Indicator Notations
**Ichimoku:**
- Tenken Sen = 'TS
- Kijun Sen = KS
- Chikou Span = CSpan'
- Senkou Span A = SSA'
- Senkou Span B = SSB'

**Bollinger Bands:**
- Lower Bollinger band = Lower boll
- Upper Bollinger band = Upper boll

**Ichi-Trader Dynamic Index (TDI):**
- RSI_Price_Line = rsig
- Market Base Line = MB
- Trade_Signal_Line2 = TSL
- Upper Band = VB High
- Lower Band = VB Low
